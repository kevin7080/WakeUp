Countries Need to Work Together to Save the Planet

What’s happening?

Some countries, like the USA and China, produce more CO₂ than others, but solving the climate crisis requires global cooperation. International agreements and collaboration are essential to reducing emissions worldwide.

How can we solve that as a group?

Countries need to come together to agree on policies that reduce CO₂ emissions, protect natural resources like forests and oceans, and promote the use of renewable energy. Global initiatives like the Paris Agreement play a crucial role in this effort.

How can I solve that as an individual?

You can make an impact by reducing your energy use, recycling, and supporting politicians and policies that prioritize climate action. Educating others and raising awareness in your community also helps create a collective effort to tackle climate change.
